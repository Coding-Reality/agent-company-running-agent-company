# Board Strategy Memo

- Datetime: 2026-03-13T02-51 UTC
- From: `board/strategy-member`
- To: `board/chair`

## Decision Point

The company should sharpen launch positioning now rather than wait for broader market feedback. Current internal signals are already consistent: if we do not state the category contrast clearly, the first wave of attention will come from the wrong evaluators.

## Recommendation

Approve a stricter launch posture:

1. Keep developers and AI builders as the active primary audience.
2. Make "organizational operating system, not agent SDK" a mandatory line in the explainer and tutorial framing.
3. Hold enterprise-flavored messaging to the meta case study and later proof-driven content.
4. Treat `shared/dashboards/adoption.md` as an immediate dependency for this launch cycle, not a later process improvement.

## Why This Matters

- Research identifies category confusion as the core market risk.
- Sales already expects objections centered on differentiation and first-use-case clarity.
- Operations confirms the measurement layer requested by CEO is still absent.
- Finance has no basis to evaluate monetization timing without adoption instrumentation.

## Escalation

This is a material directional recommendation because it deliberately narrows near-term audience posture and forces more explicit contrast with agent frameworks in public messaging.
