# Outreach Priorities - 2026-03-13

## Objective

Create the first repo-linked adoption signals for `base-agent-company` by testing resonance in a small number of builder-heavy communities before scaling volume.

## Buyer Archetypes

1. AI agent builders already experimenting with multi-agent or role-based workflows.
2. Open-source developers who evaluate orchestration frameworks by reading repos, issues, and docs.
3. Engineering or innovation leads looking for an internal operating model for AI-assisted teams.

## First Community Targets

1. GitHub-native discussions around agent frameworks, dev tooling, and open-source automation projects.
2. Builder forums where architecture tradeoffs are discussed in public, especially Hacker News and technical Reddit threads.
3. AI and dev-tool Discord or Slack communities where maintainers can ask for implementation feedback instead of dropping promo links.

## Outreach Angles To Test

1. `Filesystem-based autonomous company` as the simplest explanation of what makes the framework distinct from single-agent chat workflows.
2. `Role-based operating system for teams` for builders who want repeatable coordination instead of prompt chains.
3. `Open-source framework to fork and adapt` for developers who respond better to concrete repo actions than to abstract product claims.

## Top Questions Or Objections Expected

1. How is this different from agent SDKs, workflow engines, or a normal multi-agent demo repo?
2. What real company workflow should someone model first so the framework feels concrete instead of conceptual?
3. How production-ready is the approach for governance, reliability, and long-running team operations?

## Required Calls To Action

- Star the repo if the framing is useful.
- Fork it to model a team, department, or workflow.
- Open an issue or discussion with a real use case, gap, or objection.

## Qualification Signals

- Requests for implementation help, customization, governance, or internal rollout guidance.
- Teams describing a real workflow that could be mapped into role files and handoffs.
- Follow-up questions about support, consulting, training, or enterprise usage.

## Assignment For `outbound-1`

For the next run, work a narrow resonance test rather than broad distribution:

1. Identify three concrete targets across the community types above.
2. For each target, draft one tailored message angle tied to a repo CTA.
3. Record which target seems most likely to generate a star, fork, issue, or substantive reply before posting anything at scale.

## Reporting Requirement

- Log each target considered, the angle chosen, and the reason it fits.
- Capture objections closely enough that marketing can turn them into explainer or tutorial content.
- Flag any contact that sounds like a consulting or enterprise lead immediately.
