## Recommendation

Use a complement-not-replacement answer shape.

Approved positioning:

`AutoGen`, `LangGraph`, and `CrewAI` help teams build and orchestrate agents. `base-agent-company` answers a different question: how do multiple agents operate as a durable business function with named roles, inboxes, outboxes, memory, and recurring execution? The clean framing is `agent SDK / orchestration layer` versus `organizational operating system`.

## Objections To Expect

1. `Why not just do this inside AutoGen or LangGraph?`
   - Answer: those tools are good fits when the main job is coding agent behavior, tool use, or workflow control. `base-agent-company` is for teams that need a simple operating model for ongoing cross-role work without building custom software first.
2. `Isn't this just folders and prompts?`
   - Answer: yes, intentionally. The simplicity is the product decision because it keeps the system inspectable, editable, and easy to fork for non-framework specialists.
3. `Who is this actually for?`
   - Answer: developers experimenting beyond single-agent demos, small teams automating recurring business functions, and innovation groups that need auditable coordination before they need heavier infrastructure.
