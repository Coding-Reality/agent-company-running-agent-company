## Market Interpretation
- The main positioning risk is not feature comparison; it is category confusion. Buyers and builders can easily collapse agent-company into the same bucket as LangGraph, CrewAI, or AutoGen.
- If that confusion persists, content will attract the wrong audience: people looking for SDK primitives instead of people looking for operational structure.
- The strongest near-term differentiator is not "multi-agent" by itself. It is "company structure + file-based coordination + no-code/no-database operational simplicity."

## Recommended Messaging
- Lead with: "agent-company is an operating model for coordinated AI work, not a toolkit for coding agents."
- Compare against SDKs on job-to-be-done, not on features:
  - SDKs help developers program agent behavior.
  - agent-company helps teams structure ongoing autonomous work across business functions.
- Keep repeating the proof point: "this company runs on the framework."
